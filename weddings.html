Weddings

Wedding Venues

Your perfect day deserves a perfect setting

Villa Gardens offers four distinct wedding venues set across beautifully maintained grounds — from grand indoor halls to intimate garden spaces and open-air ceremonies. Every space can be tailored to your vision, with our dedicated events team on hand to ensure every detail is flawless.

Venue 01

The Garden Pavilion

Up to 300 guests  |  Up to 500 with tent

An elegant open-air pavilion framed by mature indigenous trees and sweeping lawns. Natural light floods the space throughout the day, making it the most sought-after setting for garden ceremonies and al fresco receptions.

  • Panoramic lawn views with garden backdrop
  • Over 1 600m² of manicured lawn
  • Adjacent welcome drinks pergola (included)
  • Private kitchen & wash-up area
  • Private restrooms & bridal suite
  • Tent hire available for larger events
Book a Viewing
Venue 02

The Grand Hall

Up to 500 guests

Our flagship wedding venue — a grand indoor hall with full climate control, soaring ceilings with exposed white beams, full-height glass windows, and a raised stage with dance floor. The ultimate setting for a lavish celebration.

  • Exposed white beam ceiling
  • Full-height glass windows & doors
  • Raised stage and dance floor
  • Climate control & full AV setup
  • Private bridal suite
  • Private kitchen & restrooms
Book a Viewing
Venue 03

The Acacia Room

Up to 80 guests

An intimate venue perfect for smaller weddings and private celebrations. The Acacia Room features an exposed whitewashed ceiling, large glass doors opening onto a 160m² veranda, and breathtaking views of the surrounding gardens.

  • 120m² main space + 160m² veranda
  • Exposed whitewashed ceiling
  • Built-in tiled bar with wooden top
  • Large glass doors & windows
  • Garden and valley views
Book a Viewing
Venue 04

The Homestead

80–150 guests

A recently renovated modern building with a warm, intimate character. The Homestead features a bar, reception area, dining room, lounge areas, and a garden terrace with pergola — ideal for multi-room wedding celebrations.

  • Bar, reception, dining & lounge areas
  • Garden terrace & pergola
  • Private kitchen & wash-up area
  • Private restrooms
  • Outdoor ceremony space adjacent
