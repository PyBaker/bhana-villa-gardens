Private Functions

Every occasion, beautifully hosted

From birthday celebrations and anniversary dinners to cocktail parties, year-end functions, and exclusive picnics under the African sky — Villa Gardens provides the space, ambience, and service to make every gathering truly memorable.

Venue 01

The Garden Pavilion

Up to 300 guests  |  Up to 500 with tent

The largest of our outdoor spaces, the Garden Pavilion is ideal for gala dinners, cocktail receptions, and large private functions. Set against a backdrop of sweeping lawns and mature trees, the atmosphere is naturally elegant.

  • Sweeping lawn & garden views
  • Adjacent welcome drinks pergola
  • Private kitchen & bar facilities
  • Dedicated restrooms
  • Tent hire available

Venue 02

The Homestead

80–150 guests

A warm, characterful venue ideal for year-end functions, birthday dinners, and private gatherings. Multiple interconnected rooms — bar, lounge, dining — give your guests the freedom to mingle in a relaxed, stylish environment.

  • Bar, lounge & dining rooms
  • Garden terrace & pergola
  • Private kitchen & restrooms
  • Warm, intimate atmosphere
Book a Viewing
Venue 03

The Acacia Room

Up to 80 guests

A refined space for intimate functions — cocktail evenings, dinner parties, and private celebrations. The built-in bar and generous veranda make The Acacia Room a natural choice for stylish, smaller gatherings.

  • 120m² main space + 160m² veranda
  • Built-in bar
  • Glass doors opening to gardens
  • Breathtaking valley views

Venue 04

Exclusive Garden Picnic

Up to 60 guests

For something truly unique — an exclusive picnic set on our indigenous wooded grounds overlooking the valley. With chairs, tables, palette furniture, and bean bags, this laid-back setting is perfect for sundowners and relaxed celebrations.

  • Wooded savannah setting
  • Valley & garden views
  • Palette furniture & bean bags
  • Tent hire available if required
  • Ideal for sundowners & cocktail events
