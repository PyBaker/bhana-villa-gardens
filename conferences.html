Conferences

Conference Facilities

Inspiring spaces for productive minds

Step away from the office and into a setting that refreshes perspective. Villa Gardens offers fully equipped conference facilities surrounded by natural beauty — the ideal environment for team retreats, corporate workshops, AGMs, training sessions, and executive meetings.

Venue 01

The Grand Hall

Up to 300 delegates

Our largest conference space — ideal for AGMs, large corporate conferences, product launches, and gala dinners. The Grand Hall features exposed white beam ceilings, full-height glass windows, and a raised stage with full audio-visual capability.

  • Raised stage & lectern
  • Full AV: projector, screens, PA system
  • Climate control
  • Private kitchen for catering
  • Private restrooms
  • Break-away areas available
Request a Package
Venue 02

The Garden Pavilion

Up to 150 delegates

A unique conference experience — the Garden Pavilion's rustic gum pole beams, whitewashed ceilings, and partial open-air design create a refreshing alternative to the typical boardroom. Ideal for team days, workshops, and mid-size conferences.

  • Rustic character with natural light
  • Projector & screen
  • Adjacent bar & pergola for breaks
  • Private kitchen & catering
  • Lawn space for team activities
Request a Package
Venue 03

The Boardroom

Up to 30 delegates

Purpose-built for executive meetings, strategy sessions, and small-group workshops. The Boardroom is set amongst young Msasa trees for a quiet, focused atmosphere — with everything you need and nothing to distract.

  • Boardroom table configuration
  • Projector, whiteboard & stationery
  • Catering & refreshments on request
  • Private, secluded setting
  • Half-day & full-day hire
Request a Package
Venue 04

The Acacia Room

Up to 80 delegates

A versatile mid-size space with views of manicured lawns and the valley beyond. The Acacia Room is well suited to training sessions, team briefings, and half-day workshops, with its large glass doors opening out to a shaded veranda.

  • 120m² main space + 160m² veranda
  • Projector & AV setup
  • Built-in bar for refreshment breaks
  • Valley & garden views
  • Flexible seating configurations
