What to expect

  • Tours typically last 45–60 minutes and cover all four venues and the grounds.
  • Available Monday to Saturday, 9:00am – 4:00pm. Saturday tours end at 11:00am.

  • Villa Gardens, off the Harare–Mutare Highway, Ruwa, Zimbabwe. Directions provided on confirmation.
